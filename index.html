Dear Mr. Aaron Mwewa Kasonde

There is something deeply moving about a person who leaves a landlocked country, crosses continents, and arrives in Moscow — not merely to study, but to carry an entire nation's questions with them. You came to Russia asking why a country as rich in copper and cobalt as Zambia still struggles to translate its resources into lasting prosperity. That question alone tells us everything about the kind of mind and heart you possess.

From your Bachelor's degree in Economics at RUDN University — one of the most culturally diverse universities in the world — to your current PhD research on foreign direct investment and Zambia's agricultural development, you have built a life shaped by curiosity, resilience, and purpose.

Goosebump Conversations was created for stories like yours — not the polished version, but the real one. The cultural shock, the growth, the identity, the moments of doubt and clarity, and the deep desire to bring something meaningful back home. I would be truly honoured to welcome you as a guest and to explore the journey behind the scholar.
